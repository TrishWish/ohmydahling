Taka Dahl

The classic. Simple dahl with red lentils and garlic.
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tab veg oil
  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • Handful fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 fresh green chilli

Method

Wash the lentils until the water runs clear.

Put lentils and water in a saucepan.

Add salt, ground coriander, turmeric, and red chilli.

Cook on medium heat for 5-10 mins. Stir occasionally. Add more water if the dahl starts to dry. Add green chilli now if you like it hot.

Once the lentils are cooked, make the Tahka:

Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until it sizzles and starts to brown slightly.

Quickly add to the dahl and cover for a minute.

Stir the dahl and add fresh coriander.

Simmer for 5 mins. Credit: Ms Zartasia Khan of Machester, London and New York
